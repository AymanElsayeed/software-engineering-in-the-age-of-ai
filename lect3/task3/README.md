- What risks exist for junior engineers?
    As companies adopt generative AI, there is a risk of a slowdown in hiring junior engineers.
    Furthermore, there is a danger that juniors may over-rely on GenAI, which could prevent them from developing necessary foundational skills.
    Dr. Yahic warns that if juniors do not develop these expert skills, it creates a 'closed loop' that leads to less efficiency and less benefit from using AI in the long term.


- How do teams adopt GenAI safely?
    teams can safely adopt Generative AI by recognizing that it is not a straightforward process and requires careful management. The key strategies:
    **Maintain Human Oversight**: The technology puts humans in the perspective, relying on them to acknowledge, filter, comment on, or redo the results produced by AI.
    **Foster Expert Knowledge**: Because AI excels in well-defined, small problems, expert architects are needed to process generated results, determine their usefulness, and understand long-term consequences.
    **Develop Context-Specific Solutions**: Since AI lacks organizational context, teams need to create dedicated teams to develop in-house methodologies, tools, and fine-tuning strategies tailored to their specific environment.
